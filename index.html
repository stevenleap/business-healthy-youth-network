Our path

Healthy Youth Network is a nonprofit based in Waterdown, Ontario, dedicated to helping young people discover their path. Through our What's Your Path program, we guide youth in refining their skills, passions, and interests to build a future aligned with their priorities and goals. Our youth-led Ally Program creates systemic change aimed at reducing gender-based violence. We believe in the power of community and the potential of every young person.
